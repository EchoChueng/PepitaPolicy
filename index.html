Privacy Policy for Pepita

At Pepita, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Pepita and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Pepita is designed to respect your privacy. To that end, we do not collect or store any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device, which means any data or information you input into Pepita remains under your control and is stored locally on your device.

Local Storage

We use local storage to save your data associated with Pepita. This information remains on your device and is not sent to any server or shared with any third parties. Local storage is used strictly to enhance your user experience by allowing you to save and retrieve your own data within the app.
